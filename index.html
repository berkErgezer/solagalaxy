About Me

Hello, my name is Aenea Vannoni. I am 23 years old and work as a cybersecurity engineer.

My hobbies are running marathons, playing piano, playing drums and writing.

For the past 10 years I have been working on a passion project of mine called 'The Grand Conquest of the Sola Galaxy'.

It is a sci fi series that will span 8 books and covers power fantasy, politics, an expansive detailed galaxy and an overarching narrative that ties many storylines together. The goal is to one day make it a tv show.

I am releasing my work slowly, I am only really able to work on this for a few days a week as my job is very stressful.

I also understand that my prose and my grammar leave much to be desired.

I appreciate any feedback and am looking for people to help me grow this project into a something huge that many people will enjoy.

Please feel free to send me an email, or draw some pictures or correct my grammar or provide an improvement of a scene or a phrase or correct some logical error I have made.

I want this to be a collaborative, community process that results in a great work of fiction for people to enjoy.
